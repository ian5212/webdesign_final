Each Work is 'Modern Art'

Discover the enchanting musical odyssey of Ian Askie, through their captivating discography. The journey begins with 'Lepidoptera,' a debut album that weaves a tale of transformation akin to a butterfly emerging from its cocoon. Following the major success of 'Lepidoptera,' Askie delves into the conceptual realms of identity and self-love with 'APOCRYPHA.' The third installment, 'EONEXPANSIAS,' propels listeners into a celestial exploration, encouraging them to reach for the stars. Concluding this captivating musical saga is 'EROS,' a profound reflection on themes of spirituality and self-discovery. Each album is a testament to Ian Askie's genre-defying artistry, with a unique message and story to tell. Immerse yourself in their musical universe, now available on Apple Music and Spotify, and let the transformative journey unfold.
