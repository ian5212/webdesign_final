'EROS'

"Immerse yourself in the soul-stirring melodies of 'EROS,' the highly anticipated fourth album by visionary artist Ian Askie. As a masterful musical alchemist, Askie delves into profound themes of identity, religious guilt, and spirituality, crafting an opus that resonates with authenticity and introspection. 'EROS' unfolds as a kaleidoscope of sonic experiences, featuring the vibrant rhythms of afrobeats, the evocative melodies of Arabic-style music, and an eclectic fusion of various other genres. Each track is a tapestry woven with the threads of Askie's artistic evolution, exploring the complexities of the human spirit with a depth that transcends musical boundaries. In 'EROS,' Ian Askie invites listeners on a transformative journey, where the rich diversity of sounds mirrors the myriad facets of the human experience. This album is not merely a collection of songs but a spiritual sojourn that invites introspection, challenging us to confront our own truths and redefine the essence of self."
