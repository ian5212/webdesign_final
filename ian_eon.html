'EON EXP ANS IAS'

"Embark on an interstellar groove with 'EONEXPANSIAS,' Ian Askie's stellar third album that boldly follows the celestial success of 'APOCRYPHA.' This cosmic journey explores the universal theme of shooting for the stars, creating an astronomical odyssey that orbits around the pursuit of goals. With tracks that twinkle like jazz constellations, groove like R&B planets, and dance through the galaxy with infectious beats, 'EONEXPANSIAS' is a musical universe where Ian Askie's talent knows no bounds. As you navigate this cosmic dance floor, let the music be your North Star, guiding you through a constellation of emotions and inspiring you to reach for new heights. In the vast expanse of musical creativity, 'EONEXPANSIAS' is a supernova of genre-blending brilliance that promises to leave listeners starry-eyed and groovin' to the rhythms of the cosmos."
