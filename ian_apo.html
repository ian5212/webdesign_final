'APOCRYPHA'

"Dive into the enigmatic and introspective world of 'APOCRYPHA,' the latest masterpiece from the visionary artist Ian Askie. This conceptual album is a mesmerizing exploration of identity and self-love, unraveling in four distinct acts that guide listeners through a transformative journey. 'ACT I: DESTRUCT' immerses you in the pulsating beats of dance music, setting the stage for the emotional odyssey that lies ahead. Transitioning seamlessly, 'ACT II: DESPAIR' unfolds with the raw intensity of hard rock, delving deep into the complexities of the human experience. The narrative evolves in 'ACT III: DESTROY,' where hyperpop and experimental sounds collide, pushing the boundaries of sonic exploration. Finally, 'ACT IV: DEFY' brings the journey full circle, wrapping you in the infectious rhythms of house music. 'APOCRYPHA' is not just an album; it's a transcendent voyage through diverse genres, each act meticulously crafted to mirror the highs and lows of self-discovery. Ian Askie's evocative storytelling and genre-defying artistry create an immersive experience that resonates with the universal themes of resilience and self-empowerment."
