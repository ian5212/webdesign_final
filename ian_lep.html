'lepidoptera'

"Get ready to embark on a transformative musical journey with 'lepidoptera,' the debut album by the enigmatic singer Ian Askie. This musical odyssey seamlessly weaves through diverse genres, from the pulsating beats of house and dance to the sultry rhythms of bossa nova, the timeless elegance of jazz, and the soulful melodies of R&B. 'lepidoptera' transcends boundaries, offering a unique blend of sounds that mirrors the multifaceted nature of the human experience. At its core, the album conveys a poignant message of introspection, guiding listeners through the labyrinth of self-discovery and the pursuit of one's first true love. Like a butterfly emerging from its cocoon, 'lepidoptera' is an exploration of metamorphosis, urging us to embrace change and emerge as beautifully transformed beings. With Ian Askie's evocative vocals and genre-defying artistry, 'lepidoptera' promises an enchanting and soul-stirring experience that resonates with the essence of personal growth and love."
