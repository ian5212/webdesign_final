Meet the prolific artist Ian Askie, a visionary who captivates hearts through their music. With four groundbreaking albums, including 'Lepidoptera,' 'APOCRYPHA,' ' EONEXPANSIAS,' and ' EROS,' Ian has etched their name in the annals of musical history. They navigate genres with a masterful touch, pouring their heart into every note to create an evocative sound that resonates with listeners. For Ian Askie, music is a powerful medium to convey messages that delve into the soul, inviting audiences on transformative journeys that transcend the ordinary. With an artistic spirit that defies definition, Ian continues to inspire and redefine the landscape of contemporary music.
